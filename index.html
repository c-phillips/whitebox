Rules (click for more info)
The object of the game is to locate the atoms ◆ by casting rays into the board.

To cast a ray, tap on one of the border squares. The result will be one of three options:
  • Hit 🗙
    A ray directly encountered an atom
  • Reflection ▉
    A ray returned to its original position (or diagonal to a border atom)
  • Deflection ⏺
    A ray passed next to an atom and bounced off
To check your results, hit the ➤ button.
